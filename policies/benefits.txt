Benefits Policy

- Health Insurance: All employees and their dependents are covered under group medical insurance.
- Provident Fund: 12% of the employee’s basic salary is contributed to the Provident Fund.
- Gratuity: Eligible after 5 years of continuous service.
- Flexible Work: Employees can avail up to 2 days per week of work-from-home.
- Professional Development: Each employee has a training allowance of ₹20,000 per year.
